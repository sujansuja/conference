About Conference

The International Conference on “Advanced Information Technology, Communication, and Energy Systems,” AITCES-2023 provide a platform for researchers and academicians to share their highly innovative ideas through paper presentation. The Conference is mainly focused on innovations in the field of Information Technology, Electronics and Communication and allied branches. This conference also provides a dais for eminent speakers from across the globe to deliver talks on emerging trends in technology. Conference Themes: AITCES-2023 covers a broad range of topics on current trends and advancement in cutting edge technologies in Electronics, Communication, Computer Sconce, IT, Electrical Engineering Sciences and allied branches. It creates an opportunity to young researchers to interact with highly experienced technocrats in their domains. Researchers and faculty from Academic Institutions, R&D Organizations, and Industry are invited to submit research papers as per the guidelines in the following link.

Publication

Manuscripts submitted will be peerreviewed, if required separately by the publisher, through a double-blind review process and accepted papers will appear in a SCOPUS-Indexed proceedings published by a reputed publisher.
Best Papers will be recognized and awarded during the Conference.

At least one author of an accepted paper must register and should present the paper in person for the paper to appear in the proceedings of the Conference.
